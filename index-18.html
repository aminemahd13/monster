Once you’ve got the right digital experience and strategy, it’s time to get your brand in front of your customers’ screens. That’s where Digital Marketing comes in. With a keen understanding of what’s happening in the digital landscape, our digital marketing experts in Malta create concepts and campaigns to make your brand succeed online. Over the years we’ve worked with brands, both large and small, to deliver campaigns that drive results. Bison Studio will design and execute effective digital marketing campaigns that will increase your visibility and make sure the right people are finding your business.

Our Digital Marketing Expertise:

SEO
Lead the pack and get your brand to the 1st page of Google with top-notch strategies that let you attract users of the world’s largest search engine.

Social Media Marketing
Project the voice of your brand through social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn and Twitter. Generate new leads and build your online presence with paid social media advertising. Reach out to the audience that matters most to your brand.

Google AdWords & PPC
Stand out amongst the big hitters in your industry. Through targeted PPC campaigns, we can reach the right people at the right time and ensure that your Google PPC ads target specific keywords, demographics and audiences.

Display Ads
Deliver attention-grabbing brand messages to boost brand awareness, traffic and sales.

Data & Analytics
Swap assumptions & hunches for data-led digital marketing. Our digital marketing experts help you make sense of analytics and reporting of any campaigns you run.

explore
the process

1

Understand

It all starts with understanding. We immerse ourselves in our client’s business to gather a good understanding and grasp onto their goals, as our client’s goals become our goals.

2

Strategy

Next comes strategy. We work with you to create the killer strategy that will lead to achieving your business goals. Data is at the heart of every decision we make in our studio, and where there is no data, we make calculated decisions based on assumption and quickly prove or disregard this based on results.

3

Execute

The most exciting part…execution. Execution is what we do on the field of play. Our studio takes on a structured approach to executing a winning strategy. To get this right, we use data to justify our decisions.

4

Evaluate

We may be excited to execute, but there is no point in any of the above if we do not evaluate. Every tactic we execute tells a story. It’s this story that gives us a deeper understanding of what tactics work best for your business and your audience.
